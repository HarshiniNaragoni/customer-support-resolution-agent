# Refund Policy

## Standard Refund
Refunds are available for items returned within 30 days of delivery. The refund amount will match the original purchase price.

## Refund Methods
- **Credit/Debit Card**: Refund to original card (5-7 business days)
- **PayPal**: Refund to PayPal account (2-3 business days)
- **Store Credit**: Immediate store credit issued upon return processing

## Partial Refunds
Partial refunds may be issued for:
- Items returned with missing accessories or packaging
- Items showing minor signs of use
- Items returned after the 30-day window but within 45 days (15% restocking fee)

## Refund Exceptions
- Shipping costs are non-refundable (unless the item was defective)
- Gift wrapping fees are non-refundable
- Custom or personalized items are non-refundable

## Late Refunds
If you haven't received your refund within 10 business days:
1. Check your bank statement again
2. Contact your credit card company
3. Contact your bank
4. If you've done all of the above and still haven't received your refund, contact us
