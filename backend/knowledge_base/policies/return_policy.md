# Return Policy

## Overview
We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not, we're here to help.

## Return Window
- **Standard returns**: 30 days from the date of delivery
- **Holiday extended returns**: Orders placed between November 15 and December 31 can be returned through January 31

## Condition Requirements
- Items must be unused and in original condition
- All original packaging, tags, and accessories must be included
- Items showing signs of wear or use cannot be returned

## How to Initiate a Return
1. Log in to your account
2. Go to "Order History"
3. Select the item you wish to return
4. Click "Request Return"
5. Print the prepaid return label
6. Ship the item back using the provided label

## Return Shipping
- **Defective items**: We cover return shipping costs
- **Change of mind**: Customer pays return shipping
- **Wrong item sent**: We cover return shipping costs

## Refund Timeline
- Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned item
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method
- Credit card refunds may take an additional 3-5 business days to appear

## Non-Returnable Items
- Personalized or custom-made items
- Gift cards
- Downloadable software
- Hazardous materials
