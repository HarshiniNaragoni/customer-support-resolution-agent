# Shipping Policy

## Shipping Options
| Method | Estimated Delivery | Cost |
|--------|-------------------|------|
| Standard | 5-7 business days | $5.99 |
| Express | 2-3 business days | $12.99 |
| Overnight | 1 business day | $24.99 |
| Free Standard | 5-7 business days | Free (orders $50+) |

## Processing Time
- Orders placed before 2:00 PM EST are processed the same day
- Orders placed after 2:00 PM EST are processed the next business day
- Weekend orders are processed on Monday

## Shipping Restrictions
- We ship to all 50 US states
- PO Boxes are accepted for standard shipping only
- Alaska and Hawaii may have extended delivery times

## Tracking
- All orders include tracking
- Tracking information is sent via email within 24 hours of shipment
- Track your order at any time through your account dashboard

## Lost or Damaged Packages
- Report lost packages within 7 days of the expected delivery date
- Report damaged packages within 48 hours of delivery
- Provide photos of damaged items for faster processing
