# Password Reset Process

## Self-Service Reset
1. Go to the login page
2. Click "Forgot Password"
3. Enter your registered email address
4. Check your email for a reset link (valid for 30 minutes)
5. Click the link and create a new password
6. Log in with your new password

## Password Requirements
- Minimum 8 characters
- Must include at least one uppercase letter
- Must include at least one lowercase letter
- Must include at least one number
- Must include at least one special character (!@#$%^&*)

## Common Issues

### Reset email not received
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Verify you used the correct email address
- Wait at least 5 minutes for delivery
- Request a new reset link if needed

### Reset link expired
- Reset links expire after 30 minutes
- Request a new reset link from the login page

### Account locked
- Too many failed login attempts will lock your account for 15 minutes
- Wait 15 minutes and try again
- Contact support if the issue persists

## Security Tips
- Never share your password with anyone
- Use a unique password for your account
- Enable two-factor authentication for added security
- Change your password every 90 days
