# FAQ

## Orders

### How do I track my order?
Log in to your account and go to "Order History." Click on the order number to see tracking details. You'll also receive tracking information via email.

### Can I cancel my order?
Orders can be cancelled within 2 hours of placement. After that, the order enters processing and cannot be cancelled. Contact support for assistance.

### How do I change my shipping address?
Contact support immediately if your order hasn't shipped yet. Once shipped, we cannot change the delivery address.

## Returns & Refunds

### How do I return an item?
Log in, go to Order History, select the item, and click "Request Return." Print the prepaid label and ship it back.

### When will I get my refund?
Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned item. Credit card refunds may take an additional 3-5 business days.

### Can I exchange an item instead of returning it?
Currently, we don't offer direct exchanges. Please return the original item and place a new order.

## Account

### How do I reset my password?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your email and follow the reset link sent to your inbox.

### How do I update my payment method?
Go to Account Settings > Payment Methods. You can add, remove, or update your saved payment methods.

## Technical Support

### My product isn't working. What do I do?
1. Check the troubleshooting guide in the product manual
2. Try resetting the device
3. Contact technical support with your order number and a description of the issue

### Do you offer technical support over the phone?
Yes, phone support is available Monday-Friday, 9 AM-6 PM EST at 1-800-SUPPORT.
