# Shipping Information

## Shipping Methods

### Standard Shipping
- Delivery time: 5-7 business days.
- Cost: Free on orders over $50, otherwise $5.99.
- Available for all 50 US states.

### Express Shipping
- Delivery time: 2-3 business days.
- Cost: $12.99.
- Available for continental US addresses.

### Overnight Shipping
- Delivery time: 1 business day.
- Cost: $24.99.
- Orders must be placed before 2 PM ET.

## Shipping Policies

### Free Shipping
- Applies to orders totaling $50 or more before tax.
- Standard shipping method only.
- Cannot be combined with other promotions.

### International Shipping
- Currently available to US addresses only.
- International shipping coming soon.

### Restricted Items
- Hazardous materials cannot be shipped.
- Oversized items may incur additional fees.
- Some items require signature on delivery.

## Delivery Issues

### Package Marked Delivered But Not Received
- Check with neighbors or building management.
- Verify shipping address is correct.
- Wait 24 hours as carrier may update status.
- Contact support if issue persists.

### Damaged in Transit
- Report within 48 hours of delivery.
- Keep original packaging for inspection.
- Provide photos of damage.
- We will send a replacement or issue refund.
