# Order Tracking Guide

## How to Track Your Order

### Step 1: Find Your Tracking Number
- Check your shipping confirmation email.
- Log into your account and view order details.
- Contact support with your order ID.

### Step 2: Use the Tracking Number
- Visit the carrier's website directly.
- Use our tracking page on the company website.
- Tracking updates may take 24-48 hours to appear.

## Tracking Status Explained

### Label Created
- Shipping label has been created.
- Package has not yet been picked up by carrier.

### In Transit
- Package is on its way to you.
- Updates show current location and next stop.

### Out for Delivery
- Package is on the delivery truck today.
- Expected delivery by end of day.

### Delivered
- Package has been delivered.
- Check delivery location and with neighbors.

## Lost Package Policy

### When to Report
- If tracking shows no movement for 7+ days.
- If estimated delivery date has passed.
- If package is marked delivered but not received.

### What We Do
- File a carrier investigation.
- Provide a replacement or refund within 5 business days.
- Carrier investigations may take 5-10 business days.
