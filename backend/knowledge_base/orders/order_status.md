# Order Status Guide

## Order Statuses

### Pending
- Order has been placed but not yet processed.
- Payment is being verified.
- Customer can cancel within 2 hours.

### Processing
- Order is being prepared for shipment.
- Items are being picked and packed.
- typically completed within 1-2 business days.

### Shipped
- Order has left our warehouse.
- Tracking number is available.
- Carrier will provide delivery updates.

### Delivered
- Order has been delivered to the customer.
- Delivery confirmation is recorded.
- Return window starts from this date.

### Returned
- Order has been returned by the customer.
- Refund is being processed.
- Items are being inspected at our facility.

## Common Order Issues

### Missing Tracking Number
- Tracking numbers are generated when the order ships.
- Processing orders may not have tracking yet.
- Check back in 1-2 business days.

### Order Delayed
- Standard shipping: 5-7 business days.
- Express shipping: 2-3 business days.
- Delays may occur due to weather or carrier issues.

### Wrong Item Received
- Contact support immediately.
- Provide order ID and description of incorrect item.
- We will arrange a replacement or refund.
