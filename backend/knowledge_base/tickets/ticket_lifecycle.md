# Ticket Lifecycle

## Ticket Stages

### Open
- Ticket has been created.
- Awaiting initial response.
- Priority determines response time.

### In Progress
- Agent is actively working on the issue.
- Customer may be waiting for information.
- Regular updates should be provided.

### Resolved
- Issue has been addressed.
- Customer has been notified.
- Ticket auto-closes after 48 hours if no reopen.

### Closed
- Ticket is permanently closed.
- No further action will be taken.
- Customer can reopen within 30 days.

### Escalated
- Ticket requires supervisor attention.
- Complex or sensitive issue.
- Customer requested escalation.

## Ticket Priority Levels

### Low
- General inquiries.
- Non-urgent questions.
- Response within 48 hours.

### Medium
- Standard support requests.
- Order issues.
- Response within 24 hours.

### High
- Urgent issues affecting service.
- Billing disputes.
- Response within 4 hours.

### Urgent
- Critical system issues.
- Security concerns.
- Response within 1 hour.

## Ticket Types

### Order Issue
- Problems with orders.
- Shipping delays.
- Wrong items received.

### Refund Request
- Refund inquiries.
- Return processing.
- Payment disputes.

### Technical Support
- Product issues.
- Setup assistance.
- Bug reports.

### Billing
- Payment questions.
- Invoice requests.
- Subscription changes.

### General
- General questions.
- Information requests.
- Feedback.

### Complaint
- Customer dissatisfaction.
- Service issues.
- Requires careful handling.
